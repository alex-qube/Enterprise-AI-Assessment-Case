# Enterprise AI Assessment Case

**Enterprise Business Analysis & AI Transformation Assessment**

NDA-safe consulting case study based on a real enterprise assessment performed during an official internship program.

The repository demonstrates a structured Business Analysis approach for assessing enterprise readiness for Digital Transformation and Artificial Intelligence while fully respecting confidentiality requirements.

---

## Executive Summary

This repository presents a real-world Business Analysis and AI Transformation Assessment conducted for a large municipal infrastructure organization.

The objective of the engagement was to understand business operations, identify operational challenges and evaluate opportunities for business process improvement before considering AI implementation.

Rather than focusing on technology, the assessment followed a business-first approach, emphasizing stakeholder interviews, business process analysis and evidence-based recommendations.

All information has been anonymized to comply with NDA requirements.

---

## Why This Repository Matters

Successful AI transformation starts with understanding the business rather than selecting technology.

This repository demonstrates a practical Business Analysis approach used to assess enterprise readiness for digital transformation before proposing AI solutions.

---

## Business Challenge

The assessment addressed several fundamental questions:

- How is the organization structured from a business perspective?
- Which business processes generate the highest operational workload?
- Where do recurring operational challenges occur?
- Which processes can be improved through conventional automation?
- Which business scenarios could benefit from Artificial Intelligence?
- How should an organization prepare for successful AI adoption?
